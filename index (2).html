Meet Sueños y Letras

Sueños y Letras is an educational project that since 2018 promotes creative freedom, critical thinking and dialogue to promote personal and community development of children and young people through reading and writing.

Read more

Since 2018 we are “Más letras, más libres”

Sueños y letras was born in 2018, in San Juan Opico municipality, department of La libertad, with a different proposal, which is accompanying children and young people in the process of discovering themselves through reading and writing, developing oral and written communication skills and awakening the ability of fantasy and get involved into transformative experiences.

Problems that our proposal solve

We have created this project to instill in students the reading and writing habit to make citizens more aware of their environment and capables to developing proposals based on their own criteria, which can only be developed through reading and writing. The project focuses on children and young people from public educational centers in El Salvador. We are convinced that they have all the potential of being agents of changes.

About

Who we are

Sueños y Letras is an educational project, led by young people, mostly university students, in which development issues are taught at a personal and community level through reading and writing. This initiative seeks to make students protagonists of positive changes at the individual and social level.

Also, it started with the mission of counteracting the use of traditionalist practices and methods that have led to mechanistic learning that can generate an environment of apathy and demotivation towards writing.

Its to objective is to develop communicative skills, analytical skills and critical thinking in young people and adolescents through reading and writing.

Sueños y Letras uses a methodology that implicates a series of interactions: readings, comments, thematic reconstruction, debates, personal elaboration and other activities that allow its participants to take greater prominence in their own educational process and come into contact with new forms of expression and different ways of seeing the world around them.

Answers to

Frequently asked questions

  • What are the areas of work of Sueños y Letras?

    Our areas of work are: Personal Development, Literature, Youth Leadership, Environment and Gender.

  • How can I be a volunteer?

    If you are interested in volunteering with us, you can contact us through social networks or fill out the following form: https://bit.ly/3OvXLAR

  • Where does Sueños y Letras work?

    Sueños y Letras was born in El Salvador and works in person in the municipalities of San Juan Opico, Ciudad Arce and Lourdes Colón in the department of La Libertad and in the municipality of Concepción Quezaltepeque in the department of Chalatenango. However, in our virtual projects we have participants from different parts of the country and the world.

  • How can I be part of a book club?

    We have several book clubs during different periods of the year. The calls to be part of the clubs are announced through our social networks, you can stay tuned to be part of the next one to be developed.

  • Do I need to have a physical book to be part of a book club?

    Not at all! We understand that not everyone has immediate access to different books, therefore, we usually manage the dynamics with PDFs and audiobooks that we provide directly to you. Otherwise, we will always inform you before starting a club.

  • Do I have to be from El Salvador to participate in a club?

    Of course not! As long as it is a virtual book club, you can be part of it from anywhere in the world. We have had Central and South American participants before, everyone is welcome to join our virtual clubs!
